About Fortcrest Technologies ltd

Fortcrest Technologies Ltd is a Cybersecurity solutions provider and Infrastructure/ Systems integrator in Kenya. Our core focus area include Systems Integration, Cybersecurity and Networking Solutions. We have partnered with top vendors in the market to ensure we provide top notch solutions in line with our clients needs and objectives. Our strategy is to uniquely place our client needs in the design phase to ensure smooth execution of the solutions to solve our client problems. A wholistic approach is guaranteed to ensure clients not only buy from us but are able to enjoy after sales services including integration, training and support. We believe in building long term relationships with our clients, helping them achieve sustainable growth and success Our team has over 10 years of experience in the industry and certified in various levels making us a trusted partner for businesses seeking a reliable and dependable company to work with..
